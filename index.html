Yubin Wang

Hi, there. I am a second-year MPhil student advised by Jun Ma and Jie Song and in Robotics under Guangzhou Pilot Scheme at The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology. I will join Huawei Noah’ Ark Lab as a young reseracher, starting from 2024 Fall.

Previously, I was a visiting student with Meriem Laleg and Hakim Ghazzai at King Abdullah University of Science and Technology. I also interned at Multi-Agent Robotic Motion Lab at National University of Singapore.

My research covers the embodied AI with the focus of integrated learning and planning for autonomous robots.

Please drop me an email if with any question or interest.
